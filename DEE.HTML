Cyberabad houses more than 100 large IT/ITeS and BFSI companies, and employs nearly one million people. The Hyderabad Knowledge City developed at Raidurgam is a new feather in the cap of this bustling area. Even though there are constant infrastructural upgrades to the area, it has been found that there is a lack of communal space for the burgeoning young population residing there. In order to provide a more engaging environment to the residents there, the Telangana Industrial Infrastructure Corporation (TGIIC) has initiated a project that will embody the spirit of Cyberabad

TGIIC is the nodal agency responsible for planning, development and maintenance of industrial infrastructure and allied facilities across the state. For this project, the TGIIC has invited consultancy proposal for architectural and transaction advisory services for development of T-Square at Raidurgam. The corporation has stated that its aim is to develop this intersection as a major tourist destination with vibrant urban spaces and entertainment hub. It will be a collaboration zone and commercial intersection to bring people together.
